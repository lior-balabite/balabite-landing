# Character Evolution Prompts — From Hats to Full Characters
### March 20, 2026
### Use in the SAME ChatGPT conversation as the existing hats

---

## Setup Prompt (say this first)

```
I want to evolve the illuminated hat characters into FULL CHARACTERS. Each hat
becomes the HEAD of a papercraft figure. The body should be made of the same
warm fabric/paper material as the hats. Style:

- Low-poly papercraft figures, same art style as the hats
- Small bodies — proportions like Rayman or Little Big Planet (big head/hat,
  smaller body, simple limbs)
- Expressive through POSTURE, not facial features — confident stance,
  leaning forward, arms crossed, pointing, carrying things
- Each character has a PERSONALITY that shows in how they stand and what
  they hold
- The hat IS the head — the eyes on the Pulse crown become the standard
  for all characters (small warm glowing dots for eyes, visible on or
  near the hat)
- Warm amber glow emanates from inside each character
- Same wooden counter/restaurant setting
- Each character should feel like someone you'd WANT on your team —
  competent, confident, alive

Show me each character individually on a clean background, full body visible,
in a pose that communicates their personality. I'll describe each one.
```

## Character 0: The Pulse — The GM

```
The Pulse: The crown hat is the head. Body is a slightly larger papercraft
figure — the leader. Standing tall, centered, calm. Arms at sides but open —
welcoming posture. A subtle glow radiates outward from the body. A small
notepad tucked under one arm. This is the GM who arrives before you and has
everything handled. Confident but not aggressive. Warm. The one you trust
completely. Eyes glowing warmly on the crown.
```

## Tips
- Generate in order (0→8) so the body style stays consistent
- After Character 0 (Pulse), say: "Use the EXACT same body proportions and
  papercraft material for all subsequent characters"
- Each character should be obviously DIFFERENT in silhouette — you should be
  able to tell them apart from a thumbnail
- The Growth Brain should have the most ATTITUDE of all — she's the one
  people will remember
- If bodies look too human, say: "More abstract, more papercraft, less
  human proportions — like a toy or figurine"
