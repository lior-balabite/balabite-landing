## What Makes the Style Work

Two references combined:
- **BODY** from `the-pulse-b.png`: mannequin joints, monochrome beige, minimal suit form
- **CROWN** from the new generation: sharper geometric origami, taller points, warm GLOWING dot eyes

Key qualities:
1. **Mannequin construction** — cylindrical joint segments at elbows, knees, wrists, ankles
2. **Monochrome body** — suit and body are the SAME warm beige/kraft tone. No color contrast.
3. **Minimal detail** — suit suggested by SHAPE only (collar, lapel fold, trouser line). No buttons, no stitching.
4. **The hat is the ONLY color/character** — everything else is neutral beige
5. **Warm glowing dot eyes** — two small luminous warm dots on the hat. Alive, confident.
6. **Soft dreamy lighting** — gentle glow around the hat, soft shadows, white background
7. **NO PROPS for individual portraits** — the hat + posture = identity. Props only in scenes.
8. **Blocky paper feet** — simple geometric shapes

**The magic is RESTRAINT.** Hat = identity. Body = neutral. Nothing else needed.

## Setup Prompt

```
I'm creating a set of 9 character illustrations for a B2B company. Each character
has a different HAT as their head — no human face, just the hat on the body.

[UPLOAD the-pulse-b.png here]

This is the BODY reference. Match this exactly for all characters:

- CONSTRUCTION: Wooden artist mannequin made of kraft paper. Visible cylindrical
  joint segments at elbows, knees, wrists, ankles. Simple geometric body parts
  connected at joints.
- MATERIAL: Everything is the SAME warm beige/kraft paper tone. The body, the
  suit, the limbs — all one monochrome material. No color contrast.
- SUIT SHAPE: Professional suit suggested by FORM only — collar shape, lapel
  fold, trouser line. NO buttons, no stitching, no texture. Just the silhouette.
- HAT = HEAD: Sits directly on the collar/shoulders. It IS the head. The hat is
  the ONLY element with color or character.
- EYES: Two small, warm, GLOWING dots on the hat. Like tiny warm lantern lights.
  Not large, not cartoonish — subtle but alive.
- LIGHTING: Soft, dreamy. Gentle luminous glow around the hat. Soft shadow beneath.
  White background.
- PROPORTIONS: Tall, elegant. 12-inch designer collectible. NOT cute or stubby.
- GENDERLESS: Every character has the EXACT same body — same build, same width,
  same proportions. No gendered shapes. No broad shoulders vs narrow. No hips, no
  chest variation. These are abstract mannequins, not people. The body is a neutral
  vessel. Only the hat and pose change between characters.
- FEET: Simple blocky paper shapes.
- NO PROPS: Characters stand with natural, confident poses. Hands at sides, or
  one hand in a gesture. No objects held. The hat alone communicates the role.

Generate each character one at a time. I'll describe the hat and pose for each.
Keep body construction, material, proportions, and lighting IDENTICAL across all 9.
Every character must have the SAME body. No variation in build or shape.
```

## Quality Checklist

For EACH individual character:
- [ ] Mannequin joint construction (cylindrical segments at elbows, knees)
- [ ] Monochrome beige body (no color contrast between suit and limbs)
- [ ] Hat IS the head, no separate face
- [ ] Small warm GLOWING dot eyes on hat
- [ ] Minimal suit detail (shape only, no buttons/stitching)
- [ ] Soft dreamy lighting with gentle hat glow
- [ ] Tall/professional proportions (NOT cute/stubby)
- [ ] Blocky paper feet
- [ ] NO PROPS (individual portraits only)
- [ ] White background, soft shadow
- [ ] Consistent scale across all 9
- [ ] Identifiable from thumbnail silhouette

---

## Troubleshooting

- **Too detailed/busy**: "Simpler. Remove everything from the hands. One monochrome
  beige tone for the body. The hat is the only feature."
- **Too cute/short**: "Taller. Adult proportions. Designer collectible, not toy."
- **Eyes too big**: "Smaller glowing dots. Subtle warm pinpoints, not large circles."
- **Too much color on body**: "The body is ONE warm beige tone. No color on the suit.
  Only the hat has color."
- **Lost mannequin joints**: "Add visible cylindrical joint segments at elbows and knees."
- **Holding things**: "Remove all props. Empty hands. Just a confident standing pose."
- **Crown too soft**: "Sharper, more geometric points. Faceted origami folds. Architectural."
- **Style drifting**: Upload the-pulse-b.png again and say "Match THIS body. Same
  material, same proportions, same lighting. Only change the hat."
