## Quality Checklist

Before accepting any image as a production asset, verify:

- [ ] Paper texture is photorealistic (visible grain, fold lines, slight imperfections)
- [ ] Eyes are consistent (two bronze dots, small, with light reflection)
- [ ] Crown glow is warm amber (not white, not yellow-neon)
- [ ] Background is warm cream, not stark white
- [ ] Counter/surfaces are warm wood, consistent across images
- [ ] Lighting is warm amber/golden, from the left
- [ ] No text appears in the image (except natural paper textures)
- [ ] No wax seals on characters — characters are identified by eyes and paper texture only
- [ ] Scale is consistent (hats = espresso cup size)
- [ ] The image "feels warm" — gut check
