## The Principle

**Only the Crown has eyes.** It is the soul, the consciousness, the Ba'al HaBayit.

The 8 specialist hats are its **tools** — beautiful, alive through inner glow and posture, but faceless. They convey intelligence through:
- **Inner glow** — a faint warm light through the paper grain (the AI is awake inside)
- **Posture/tilt** — leaning forward (analyzing), tilted (energetic), low and steady (grounded)
- **Action** — each hat is CAUGHT DOING ITS JOB, interacting with objects, not just sitting there
- **Paper texture** — the data they're made from IS their identity

This is the **Pixar lamp approach**: no face, pure personality through form, light, and action.

### The Fig.security Formula
Fig uses: **Different animal shapes + Same checkered uniform = Family**
We use: **Different hat shapes + Same warm restaurant paper family = Family**

All hats are folded from the same WORLD of warm cream/off-white restaurant paper. Distinction comes from **silhouette** (primary) and **printed content on the paper** (secondary). The crown is the only dramatically different color (gold).

## Instructions

### Workflow
1. Paste your approved hero image (P1-01 crown) into the ChatGPT conversation
2. Paste this style lock:
> "Use this EXACT style for all future images. Match the lighting, paper texture, fold detail, color warmth, and overall mood precisely. IMPORTANT RULE: Only the golden crown has eyes. All other hats have NO EYES, NO FACE, NO DOTS. They are beautiful paper objects alive through inner glow and posture only. This rule applies to every image from now on."
3. Generate each character (C0–C8) in order below
4. After all 9 are approved, run the Recombination Prompt (R1) at the bottom

### Critical Style Rules
- **NO EYES on hats (C1–C8)**. No dots, no faces. Beautiful paper objects that glow from within.
- **EYES only on the Crown (C0)**. Two tiny bronze pinpoint dots. The only face.
- **Unified paper family**: All hats are warm cream/off-white restaurant paper. Subtle tone variations only (slightly grey for newspaper, slightly yellow for tickets). NOT dramatically different colors.
- **"Caught in the act"**: Every hat is mid-action, interacting with objects — not sitting passively beside props.
- **Job-specific backgrounds**: Each solo shot shows a different part of the restaurant.
- **Shared element**: Every hat has a small wax seal (circle with a bite taken out).

## Correction Templates

### If ChatGPT Adds Eyes to Hats
> "REMOVE the eyes from all hats. Only the golden crown has eyes. The hats are NOT creatures — they are beautiful paper objects alive through inner glow and posture. No dots, no faces, no expressions on any hat. Regenerate."

### If Hats Look Too Similar
> "The hats are too similar. Each should be a different SILHOUETTE — the toque is tall and pleated, the fedora has a pinched crown, the safari has a wide brim, the bandana is flat. Exaggerate the shape differences. They should be identifiable as silhouettes at 32px."

### If Paper Colors Diverge Too Much
> "The hats should all feel like they're from the same family of warm cream restaurant paper. Only the crown is gold. Pull the colors back into the warm cream family — subtle tone differences only (slightly grey for newspaper, slightly yellow for tickets, slightly white for receipts)."

### If a Hat Isn't "Doing" Anything
> "The [hat name] should be CAUGHT IN THE ACT of [specific action]. Not sitting passively — it should be mid-action, interacting with objects. Show the moment frozen."
