## The Big Idea: AI Specialists as Characters

Instead of Fig's animals, BalaBite's visual world is built around **AI specialist characters** — each one representing a domain of the restaurant:

### The Cast

| Specialist | Domain | Color | Personality |
|-----------|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Menu Specialist** | Menu intelligence, pricing, descriptions | Amber/Gold | The perfectionist — obsesses over every dish position |
| **Revenue Specialist** | Finance, avg check, anomalies | Emerald/Green | The accountant — sees every dollar, spots problems first |
| **Operations Specialist** | Ticket times, staffing, performance | Purple | The floor manager — watches everything, misses nothing |
| **Customer Specialist** | Loyalty, churn, VIPs, preferences | Blue | The host — remembers every face and allergy |
| **The Pulse** | Central brain, morning briefing, coordination | Amber-to-purple gradient | The boss — synthesizes everything, delivers the briefing |

### Visual Identity Options (needs designer/illustrator)

1. **Abstract orb-beings** — each specialist is a glowing orb with their color, floating around the Pulse. Minimal, on-brand with current orb.
2. **Illustrated characters with personality** — Fig-style but restaurant-themed. Chef hats, aprons, tools. Each specialist has a distinct silhouette.
3. **Geometric faces/masks** — the Arena AI "4 hats" concept visualized as mask-like icons. More abstract, more tech.
4. **Minimal line-art characters** — Anthropic-inspired hand-drawn style, consistent with existing icon system.

### Interactive Moments (can code these)

- **Morning briefing scroll experience** — as user scrolls, the Pulse delivers insights one by one, each specialist chiming in
- **"Don't touch the menu"** — Fig-inspired: a button that says "DO NOT rearrange this menu" — when clicked, the Menu Specialist shuffles everything and shows the revenue impact
- **Specialist cards that respond** — hover over a specialist, it shows a live-style insight animation
- **The Pulse greeting** — type your name, the Pulse says "Good morning, [name]. Here's what your AI partner found."

## Technical Design Principles (from research)

### What premium sites do:
1. **One accent color** — amber/gold for BalaBite, everything else is neutrals
2. **Product in the hero** — screenshot, video, or interactive demo above the fold
3. **Sticky nav with CTA** — Features, How It Works + "Get Early Access" always visible
4. **Light OR dark, not both** — pick one. Current: dark. Fig proves light works too.
5. **Real social proof** — testimonials > metrics. Names > numbers.
6. **Massive whitespace** — Bold proves restraint = premium
7. **Interactive surprise** — one moment that makes people screenshot and share
8. **Typography hierarchy** — big bold headlines, lightweight body text

### Current gaps to fix:
1. No product visibility anywhere on page
2. Nav has no menu items or CTA
3. Too many accent colors (amber, purple, blue, green, emerald)
4. No testimonials or real social proof
5. No interactive/memorable moment
6. Phone mockup placeholder still empty
7. No video or demo
