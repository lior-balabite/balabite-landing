### March 18, 2026

## The 9 Brains

### The Pulse (Brain 0) — The Command Center
- **Role**: CEO/COO proxy. Orchestrator. The "Good morning, Chef."
- **Mission**: SAVE TIME — everything that happened, in 30 seconds, every morning
- **Owns**: Morning briefings, weekly reports, action queue (Approve/Dismiss), predictions, Spotify Wrapped annual story
- **Gen 2**: Proactive daily briefing from all brains
- **Gen 3**: Narrative storytelling ("This week at your restaurant..."), predictive day planning
- **Gen 4**: Fully autonomous decision-making

---

### MAKE MONEY

#### Menu Brain (Brain 1) — The Product Strategist
- **Role**: Head of Product. What to sell, where, at what price.
- **Mission**: MAKE MONEY — "3 items on your menu are losing you money"
- **Owns**: Arena AI (4-hat analysis: Menu Engineer, Culinary Director, Psychology, Kitchen Ops), pricing strategy, menu positioning, descriptions, health scores, what to add/cut
- **Gen 2**: Arena AI multi-perspective analysis
- **Gen 3**: Dynamic pricing (time/demand/weather), auto-seasonal menus, trend-based suggestions
- **Gen 4**: Auto-adjusts menu daily based on demand
- **Purple Cow**: Nobody analyzes a single dish from 4 expert perspectives

#### Guest Brain (Brain 2) — The Front of House Star
- **Role**: FOH Manager + Customer Success. Everything the guest touches.
- **Mission**: MAKE MONEY — "AI waiter that upsells every table in 15 languages"
- **Owns**: AI waiter, 4 menu modes (Grid/Stories/Discover/Chat), 15+ languages, allergen safety, floor/tables/seating, upsells, food pairings, multi-guest ordering, Noted saves
- **Gen 2**: AI waiter with conversational ordering
- **Gen 3**: Deep guest memory across visits ("welcome back Sarah, your usual Negroni?"), unreasonable hospitality automation, "she said the pasta was too salty last time"
- **Gen 4**: Fully autonomous guest experience
- **Purple Cow**: Will Guidara's unreasonable hospitality, automated. The guest feels KNOWN.

#### Growth Brain (Brain 3) — The Marketing Director
- **Role**: Head of Marketing. Fill seats, bring them back, protect the brand.
- **Mission**: MAKE MONEY — "28% more repeat visits"
- **Owns**: Campaigns, loyalty programs, reviews/reputation management, promotions, contests, digital presence, social content
- **Gen 2**: Campaigns, review monitoring, loyalty
- **Gen 3**: Social listening, viral moment capture, auto-content from real data, response drafting
- **Gen 4**: Autonomous marketing campaigns

#### Market Brain (Brain 4) — The Scout
- **Role**: Head of Business Intelligence. Everything OUTSIDE your four walls.
- **Mission**: MAKE MONEY — "2,300 people near you searched for something you don't serve"
- **Owns**: Competitor intelligence (pricing, reviews, openings, menu changes), demand signals (Google search trends, delivery platform data, social trends), local intelligence (events, weather, demographics, foot traffic, new construction), industry trends
- **Gen 2**: Competitor watching, price tracking
- **Gen 3**: Demand signals, search trend analysis, delivery platform data, local event intelligence
- **Gen 4**: Predictive market positioning
- **Purple Cow**: Google-level market intelligence for a $2M/year restaurant. Nobody offers this.

---

### STOP LOSING MONEY

#### Kitchen Brain (Brain 5) — The Chef's Right Hand
- **Role**: Chef + Purchasing Director. Everything from ingredient to plate.
- **Mission**: STOP LOSING MONEY — "Cut waste from 8% to 4%"
- **Owns**: Inventory, recipes, prep, waste tracking, vendor ordering, par levels, food cost, KDS (4 view modes), course timing, dietary flags, equipment
- **Gen 2**: Inventory management, waste tracking, auto-ordering, KDS
- **Gen 3**: Predictive prep, auto-86 when inventory hits threshold, auto-generate daily specials from expiring inventory, line rebalancing when cook calls out
- **Gen 4**: Autonomous kitchen operations
- **Note**: Supply chain (purchasing, vendors, par levels) lives here, not in Finance

#### Finance Brain (Brain 6) — The CFO
- **Role**: CFO/Controller. Where money goes and where it leaks.
- **Mission**: STOP LOSING MONEY — "Found $2,400/month you didn't know you were losing"
- **Owns**: P&L, cash flow, margins, tip distribution (penny-perfect), payroll reconciliation, vendor invoices, anomaly detection, what-if simulator ("what if I raise prices 8%?")
- **Gen 2**: Reporting, tip calculator, anomaly flags, simulator
- **Gen 3**: Revenue maximization (table turn optimization, daypart optimization, channel optimization — "remove these 4 items from DoorDash, margin is negative"), server upsell tracking
- **Gen 4**: Autonomous financial management

---

### SAVE TIME

#### Team Brain (Brain 7) — The HR Director
- **Role**: Head of People. Right people, right time, right morale.
- **Mission**: SAVE TIME + SAVE MONEY — "Schedule 20 staff in 30 seconds. Predict who's leaving."
- **Owns**: Scheduling, retention prediction ("Maria's tips dropped 18% — burnout risk"), performance tracking, labor cost optimization, shift swaps, hiring pipeline
- **Gen 2**: Auto-scheduling, retention prediction, performance metrics
- **Gen 3**: AI performance coaching, training modules, culture scoring, predictive staffing based on demand forecasts
- **Gen 4**: Autonomous HR

#### Voice Brain (Brain 8) — The 24/7 Receptionist
- **Role**: The phone answerer. Always on.
- **Mission**: SAVE TIME → MAKE MONEY — "We answer your phone. You stop losing reservations."
- **Owns**: Phone answering 24/7, reservation capture, to-go orders, FAQ handling ("do you have gluten-free?"), missed-call recovery, after-hours
- **Gen 2**: Voice AI for reservations and basic questions
- **Gen 3**: Sentiment detection during calls, proactive outreach calls ("Hi, we noticed you haven't visited in a while..."), multi-platform (phone + WhatsApp + Instagram DMs)
- **Gen 4**: Full voice concierge

## Multi-Unit Note
Multi-unit is a LENS, not a brain. Every brain operates across locations:
- Menu Brain: "Downtown location sells 3x more pasta than suburban — here's why"
- Finance Brain: "Location 7's food cost is 6% above group average"
- Team Brain: "Compare server performance across all 15 locations"
- The Pulse: "3 locations need attention this morning. Priority order."

## Landing Page Strategy

**Entry point**: The Pulse (the morning briefing STORY, not a feature pitch)
**Lead with anxiety, not brains**: Each section opens with a pain → the relevant brain resolves it
**Three-pillar structure**: Make Money / Stop Losing / Save Time
**Tease Gen 3**: Show Gen 2 features, hint at Gen 3 capabilities as "coming" or "learning"

### Purple Cow Features for the Landing Page
1. "Good morning, Chef" — proactive daily briefing (nobody does this)
2. Arena AI — 4 expert hats on every dish (nobody does this)
3. Guest memory — "welcome back, Sarah" across visits (unreasonable hospitality, automated)
4. Demand signals — "2,300 people searched for X near you" (Google-level intelligence)
5. Dynamic pricing — airline pricing for restaurants (Gen 3 tease)
6. Spotify Wrapped for restaurants — annual story (Gen 3 tease)
7. Interactive "DO NOT touch" moment on the website (Fig-style brand magic)
